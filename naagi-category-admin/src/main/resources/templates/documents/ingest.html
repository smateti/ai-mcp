Tips
  • PDF files: Text is extracted automatically. Scanned PDFs may not work well.
  • Word documents: Both .doc and .docx formats are supported.
  • Markdown files: Converted to plain text while preserving structure.
  • Text files: Ingested directly as-is.
  • The document title must be unique
  • Large documents will be automatically split into smaller chunks
  • Categories help filter results when querying specific knowledge domains
